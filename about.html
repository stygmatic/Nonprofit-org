Who We Are

RoboFuture Africa is a nonprofit organization focused on empowering the children of Africa with the skills and knowledge of robotics, paving the way for a technologically advanced and prosperous future.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child in Africa has access to education in robotics and technology, enabling them to be innovators and leaders of the future.
